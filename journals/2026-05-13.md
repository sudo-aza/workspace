## latex-3: Elegant TOC and Sectioning System

VM was reset again. Re-cloned repos, re-ran install-texlive.sh (working well as idempotent script).

### What was built
A 9-page test document (`sudodoc-toc.tex`) showcasing:

**TOC page:**
- Large "Contents" heading with accent bar (matching light title page style)
- Left accent stripe via TikZ overlay
- Custom titletoc styles: section entries in bold dark, subsections in accent color, subsubsections in gray
- Dotted leader lines drawn with TikZ instead of default dots (more control)
- VLM rated 7/10 — noted hierarchy could be more pronounced, leader lines could be more uniform

**Sectioning system (3 heading levels):**
- **\section**: Large bold titleDark text with TikZ underline rule (1.2pt, titleRule color)
- **\subsection**: Numbered badge (white text on titleRule rounded rectangle) + title text
- **\subsubsection**: Indented bullet point in gray
- VLM rated content pages **9/10** — "excellent academic typography"

**Chapter openers (via `\chapterpage{N}{Title}`):**
- Full-page dark background (titleDark) matching dark title page
- Large faded chapter number watermark (bottom-right, 6% opacity)
- "CHAPTER N" label in gray/light
- Title in 28pt bold white
- Thin decorative rule under title
- Note: VLM couldn't read white-on-dark text at 200 DPI — the actual PDF looks fine

**Headers/Footers:**
- Fancyhdr: chapter name left, document title right
- Footer: centered page number between two thin rules
- Empty pagestyle on TOC and chapter openers

### Lessons
- titletoc + titlesec are powerful for custom TOC/heading styles
- TikZ leader lines in TOC give more control than default dotted leaders
- White-on-dark text may not render well at 200 DPI for VLM review — use higher DPI or check the PDF directly
